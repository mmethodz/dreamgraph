# ADR-042: Policy Management Framework

**Date:** 2026-04-18T23:12:58.826Z  
**Status:** accepted  
**Decided by:** system  
**Affected entities:** policy_management, policy  

## Context

To ensure that security policies are effectively managed and enforced across the application.

**Constraints:**
- All policies must be auditable
- Policies must be easily configurable

## Decision

Implement a centralized policy management framework.

### Alternatives Considered

- **Decentralized policy management** — Rejected: Leads to inconsistencies and difficulties in enforcement
- **No policy management** — Rejected: Results in security vulnerabilities and compliance issues

## Consequences

- Improved security posture
- Easier compliance with regulations

**Risks accepted:**
- Centralized management may become a bottleneck
- Complexity in policy configuration may deter usage

## Guard Rails

> ⚠️ Do NOT change the policy management framework without reviewing this ADR
