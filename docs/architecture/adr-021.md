# ADR-021: Instance Management Strategy

**Date:** 2026-04-18T23:11:34.866Z  
**Status:** accepted  
**Decided by:** system  
**Affected entities:** instance_management, instance_registry  

## Context

To effectively manage the lifecycle of instances within the application.

**Constraints:**
- Instances must be isolated from each other

## Decision

Implement a centralized instance management hub.

### Alternatives Considered

- **Decentralized instance management** — Rejected: Increases complexity and risk of conflicts.
- **Single instance only** — Rejected: Limits scalability and flexibility.

## Consequences

- Improved management and monitoring of instances.

**Risks accepted:**
- Centralized management could become a bottleneck.

## Guard Rails

> ⚠️ Do NOT change the instance management strategy without reviewing this ADR.
