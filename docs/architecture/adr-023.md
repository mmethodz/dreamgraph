# ADR-023: Policy Management Framework

**Date:** 2026-04-18T23:11:34.901Z  
**Status:** accepted  
**Decided by:** system  
**Affected entities:** policy_management, policy_enforcement  

## Context

To ensure that policies are consistently enforced across the application.

**Constraints:**
- All policies must be auditable and enforceable

## Decision

Adopt a centralized policy management framework.

### Alternatives Considered

- **Decentralized policy management** — Rejected: Increases complexity and risk of non-compliance.
- **No formal policy management** — Rejected: Leads to arbitrary enforcement and potential security risks.

## Consequences

- Improved compliance and security across the application.

**Risks accepted:**
- Centralized management could slow down policy updates.

## Guard Rails

> ⚠️ Do NOT change the policy management framework without reviewing this ADR.
